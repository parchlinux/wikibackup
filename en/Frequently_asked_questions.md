## General Questions

### What is Parch?

Parch is a distribution of the GNU/Linux operating system developed by the Parch team for various platforms, including personal computers with different CPU architectures and mobile phones.  
As an Iranian Linux distribution, the Parch project aims to provide a user-friendly, stable, and secure operating system for Iranian users.  
It is designed based on the specific needs of Persian-speaking users and strives to offer a suitable environment for everyday tasks such as office work, education, and entertainment, while also contributing to the growth of the open-source technology community in Iran.

---

### Why should I use Parch?

Parch Linux is a minimal and fast distribution based on Arch Linux, designed for developers, advanced users, and those who need full control over their system.  
It maintains the simplicity and flexibility of Arch while offering a more optimized, lightweight experience with practical defaults for both mobile and desktop versions.  
It is actively developed by a dedicated team focusing on stability and compatibility with modern needs.

---

### Which architectures does Parch support?

Currently, Parch actively supports the **x86_64** architecture.  
ARM and RISC-V architectures are also under development.

---

### I’m new to GNU/Linux. Is Parch suitable for me?

Yes, Parch can be a good option for you.  
Although it is based on Arch, the installation and usage experience has been simplified.  
If you are interested in learning and exploring the Linux world, Parch can be both educational and powerful.

---

### When will a new version be available?

Parch is a **rolling release distribution**, meaning it is always up to date.  
Every time you update the system, you get the latest package versions.  

Additionally, every **45 days** a new ISO build is published on the website so that new users don’t face a large number of updates after installation.

---

### Which desktop environments does Parch support?

By default, Parch is shipped with **GNOME** and **Plasma** desktop environments.  
Users can install any other desktop environment they prefer based on their needs.

---

## Installation Questions

### Does Parch have a graphical installer?

Yes. Parch provides a graphical installer based on **Calamares**.

---

### Which desktop environment or window manager should I use?

You can choose between **GNOME** and **Plasma** depending on your needs.  
To see the differences between the two, you can refer to the comparison page.
